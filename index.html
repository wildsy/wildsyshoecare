    About Us

    Wildsy Shoe Care

    Wildsy Shoe Care is a business engaged in special shoe washing services. Wildsy Shoe Care was founded on March 2 2023 which can be regarded as a college student pioneer of shoe laundry in Bekasi and Purwakarta, starting from an assignment for an entrepreneurship and advanced programming course at a university in the city of Bandung.

    Services

    We provide a wide range of services for the care of your favorite items that will be carried out by our experienced and professional team.

    Fast Cleaning

    Fast cleaning is an instant washing of the upper and midsole which can be waited for 15-30 minutes.

    Hard Cleaning

    Detailed and thorough shoe cleaning care for all parts.
